About

Coming from a programming background, I became involved in website development while working as a Senior Analyst Programmer at Celona Technologies in 2002. I learnt HTML, CSS and JavaScript at an SROCN accredited workshop course at Guildford College. I followed this up by attending an in-house html and css course at Celona, then went on to maintain the company's website.

In January 2006, having left Celona, I began writing Websites for the charity and voluntary sector. Then in July 2006 I joined the Financial Times to work on the FT.com website.

In March 2009 I returned to freelance work, as a front end web developer doing work for Reed Business Information, then Allianz Insurance in Guildford.
