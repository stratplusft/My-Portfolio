For smaller companies, voluntary organisations and charities, I can design and build either static or content managed (CMS) websites.

My most recent work was to create a new WordPress responsive child theme for the Royal High School club in London

Previously I have written sites for Citizens Advice Bureaux and Scots in London.

As a freelance Front End Web Developer based in Surrey, specialising in hand coded HTML and CSS, I have been working in the enterprise environment on Allianz Insurance Quote and Buy Home and Motor websites at Allianz Insurance in Guildford, and before that at Reed Business Information on their various websites associated with their publications, including Farmers Weekly, Computer Weekly and Roadtransport.com .

My work at Allianz included reskinning the site to run the BMW and Mini car insurance quote and buy websites.

Previously I was employed by the Financial Times from 2006 to 2009, as a member of the team developing and maintaining the main FT web site www.ft.com.

Key skills are HTML5, XHTML, CSS, JQuery, JavaScript, Ajax, XML, XSL, Eidos-Media Methode CMS, Photoshop CS5, Fireworks CS5, Balsamiq Mockups, with knowledge of Java, PHP5, Perl, Episerver, WordPress and Eclipse.
